Design Philosophy & Skillset

My Skills

User Research

I love using the 5 second usability testing. It is fast yet effective. It analyzes user’s initial feelings. I use the 5 second test on myself to make sure my content and components work well.

User Empathy

I enjoy creating and using user persona and card sorting. It gives me an idea of how people feels and think. With the card sorting, it shows how users are really thinking, their expectations of how my websites and apps should function.

Qualitative Interview

I use an interview plan identifying the research problem and building research objectives to create interview questions.
