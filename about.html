Design Philosophy
& Skillset

With only 4 contact points (head, wrist, body, throat) to score in Kendo, you have to be creative to score those points. Creativity is formed from limitations and constraints. Some of the most successful websites are simple ones that does it purpose well e.g. Google, Amazon, YouTube, Netflix and Wikipedia. I believe that the beauty lies in making things simple and easy to use. The beauty in design.

Iterative Design involves continuous testing and refining based on feedback. In Kendo the concept of Keiko (practice) embodies continuous improvement. Practitioners constantly refine their techniques and strategies through regular training, similar to iterative processes in UX.
